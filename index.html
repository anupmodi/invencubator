Ethos

What's in a artisan product?

  • Beautiful Patterns
  • Intricate details
  • The people who make it, The people who make it, The people who make it

We began sourcing from small number of skilled artisans, working from one of co-founder's garage and artisans are still just as integral to every product we source for you. The artisan is an important factor in the equation of society and culture. The Village communities of craftsman have always been the center of the traditional crafts. They are not only artisans, they have wonderful qualities within them. Some of them are wonderful leaders, quality controllers, mentors.

Since begning we have placed artisans first, providing education and literacy programs and giving artisans the chance to rise up to new heights. They never though in wildest dreams that someday they would be able to read and write. They have now confidence that they can do anything in the world! It is a very inspirational statement for us and challenge for us that how can we help them to grow with the organization. We continue to provide a sustainable living to those who may otherwise struggle. Artisans with bad eyesight can't work with lighter colors so we ask them to make products in higher contrast patterns so they can continue to work. They are thankful to our customers who ask us to work on simpler designs so that artisans are able to sustain work, and continue to enjoy it. With your support artisans have been able to transform their lives. A lot of artisans are now making multiple times as much they did working for contractors. They are finally able to pay for children's education, extension of house and supporting family. Some young artisans are primary earner in family since father's passing. They are now able to pay for all of sisters and brothers through college.

ACube World lays importance in keeping family values alive. The first step in this process is empowering every woman of rural India by giving her access to a sustainable livelihood at her doorstep. ACube World also source from skilled artisans who live in the most remote parts with little to no access to transport. The greatest challenge in these artisans’ lives is commuting to work and back, often resulting in families that live far apart. One of the key differentiation of the ACube World model is an artisan’s ability to not only work from home but have business travel to them. We realized, working with the artisans, when a customer buys an artisan product, actually he is not just buying the product but he is also helping an artisan family with education, health care and a better life.

ACube World has trained many artisans from scratch.
There are now many woman enrolled in our education programs.
Our health camps care for many artisans across India.
We provide life insurance and scholarships for many artisans.

About Us

ACube World was founded by professionals who left behind their flourishing corporate careers and life in Silicon Valley USA and returned to India. Each one came from a different functional background and they put their heart, money and soul into creating a startup organization that could enable small and medium designers, entrepreneurs, boutiques and stores to source or get made unique products from India which are artisan made, fair-trade, environment friendly, intensive with unique heritage Indian techniques and a process that minimizes wastage in production.

We take up each task as an appropriate “project” and execute it like we did in our corporate jobs in Silicon Valley! We aspire to be one stop shop to provide for an efficient, time-bound delivery and thereby saving our business clients personal cost and time in accomplishing their goals.

What is our Vision?

We specialize in bringing unique artisan made products to the world! We strive to build a business and a personal relationship based on core values of trust, integrity, longevity and understanding with our partners and clients.

We understand that an international buyer - who may not speak our language, or may have never visited the country - maybe skeptical about doing business for the first time with a stranger. Our Ethos

We work with factories practicing Fairtrade. Fair trade factories pay fair wages to their employees, and also create long-term, direct trading relationships based on dialogue, transparency, equity and respect. Fair trade uses a fair system of exchange to empower producers to develop their own businesses and to foster sustainable development. We follow a set of internationally-accepted fair trade principles and practices that are designed to improve the livelihood of low-income people through alternative trade. They are:

  • Transparency and long-term relationships
  • Commitment to fair pay for labor
  • Capacity building for long-term impact
  • Equal opportunity for women
  • Concern for the environment
  • Respect for cultural identity
  • Reasonable working conditions
  • No child exploitation

We aim to create a community by bringing artisans and cottage industry manufacturers on the global map by helping them increase their potential and creativity by working with international buyers and designers to create unique products. We are enablers to the outside world for doing business in India with ease and passion. Having lived overseas for two decades we realize the importance of timeliness, communication and transparency. We are here to provide end-to-end solutions to our customers - from story boarding to designing their concepts, production, packaging and photography. All our customers need to do is focus on their brand marketing and sales - rest all is taken care of by us!

Few specifics:

  • Women empowerment: We work with several not-For-profit organizations that employ, empower and generate skills development for women in rural areas. Our upcycled fabric toys, handmade jewelry, embroideries, hand painted scarves are made by women of these organizations. We organize training camps for them as required and bring their craftsmanship to the world by offering it to our international buyers and bringing them to a global platform of equal opportunity.
  • Zero-Waste and upcycling: In an effort to avoid harming the planet, we work with artisans and factories to ensure that every left-over material from production is reused in making our in-house designed upcycled products such as soft toys, handcrafted fabric jewelry or carry bags and hair accessories. We design and create new products from remnant materials, so they don’t end up in landfills. Creativity is key to upcycling. Our designers work side-by-side with the production team to upcycle and incorporate even the tiniest scraps into something beautiful. Our excess fabric strips are tediously hand cut and individually sewn back into making patchwork fabric.
  • Azo-Free & Hand-block Printing: We mostly work with hand-block or hand-screen printing for all our customers. This is the most original technique for printing and we want to bring that element to all our customers!
  • Hand-block Carvings: If you are an artist or a designer, we can convert your art pieces into hand print blocks! We work with experienced artisans who only do block carvings.
  • Low Minimum Order Quantities (MOQ): We started our company to provide solutions to those who want low quantity commitments - small boutique buyers, starting out entrepreneurs or even established medium and large size stores. We see ourselves as facilitators and enablers for the global buyer who wants a piece of India.

Our production process starts out with receiving a design from our buyers - in just a sketch form or an image or even a concept that they are describing – we then finalize the design and get blocks carved for the design. Once that is done, the blocks are passed on to the block-printing artisanal units where they conduct the printing process by hand. This is a lengthy process and requires patience, practice and precision. Some of the block printing artisans have been doing this for up to fifty years and are experts who then pass on their valuable skills - it’s a family tradition that’s taught and passed on through generations.

For each of our product we have different artisan groups who specialize and work on that product only. So, what we provide our customers are specialized unique high quality products. We have gone a step further to provide end-to-end solution to our buyers. So, we also offer complete packaging solutions - fabric, jute, juco, handmade-paper, recycled-paper and other such packaging solutions along with branding hang-tags for their products. We also get photoshoots done for our buyers, so they can immediately start selling their products online or start preparing their look-book(s)/catalogue(s) while the goods are being shipped to them. Besides, it also proves to be more cost effective!

Our hand-block premium cotton range of baby and kids product range (nursery, bedding, diapering, clothing & beachwear) along with matching resort-wear (kaftan, tunics, t-shirt, robes, men’s shirt, scarves, stoles and slings) for mums and dads.

Again, our specialty is working in hand-block and hand-screen printed cotton fabrics, azo-free printing and practicing zero-wastage in production; our production wastage fabric is upcycled to create beautiful artisan jewelry, key chains, bag charms, hair accessories and plush toys. We have got special Upcycled Fabric Keychains made from our production wastage fabric as giveaways at our stall to visitorswhich are a big hit with all our global buyers!!!.

We hope to be enablers to every dreamer, artist and entrepreneur!
